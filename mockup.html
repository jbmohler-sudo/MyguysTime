Payroll Handoff

What happens after "this is their check amount"?

My Guys Time calculates the numbers. Here's how to get them to your CPA, your payroll service, or your own check register.

Pick your path
Brainstorm note: You don't need to build check printing. That becomes a payroll processor business — licenses, compliance, the whole nightmare. Your job is to get the right number into the right person's hands. These 3 paths cover 95% of your users.
📊
Export CSV for your accountant
Best for: CPA does your payroll in QuickBooks or by hand
Most common

One click generates a clean CSV with each worker's name, hours, gross, withholding, reimbursements, and net check. Your CPA drops it straight into QuickBooks. This is already built.

Import into Gusto, ADP, or Paychex
Best for: companies already using a payroll service
Future

These services all accept CSV uploads. If you know what columns Gusto/ADP expects, we can format the export to match — making My Guys Time a direct feeder.

ADP Workforce CSV

✋
Pay by hand (cash, check, Zelle)
Best for: small crews who pay direct
Very common

A lot of guys in the trades just need to know the number without sending anything anywhere. Give each person a clear "write this check for $___" view. Printable pay stub that's clean enough to hand over.

⛔ What you don't need to build (and why)
✗Direct deposit — requires ACH processor license and banking partnerships
✗Check printing — MICR ink, check stock, printer setup — your users' banks or QBO already do this
✗Tax filing — W-2s, 940s, 941s — that's a licensed payroll processor job, not yours
✗Full payroll engine — that's Gusto's $40/mo product; your $20 bridge wins on simplicity

Reporting Cadence

What your CPA needs and when

Weekly exports confirm the paycheck. Monthly and quarterly summaries keep the tax calendar from biting you.

⚠ Liability boundary My Guys Time tracks hours and estimates taxes. It does not file 940s, 941s, or any tax form. Always verify totals with your CPA before any tax payment.
📋
Weekly export
Every Friday or after you approve crews

Confirms what you paid. Your CPA uses this to post payroll entries. Already built — this is your current CSV export.

Includes: Each worker · Hours · Gross · Federal WH est. · State WH est. · Reimbursements · Net check
📆
Monthly rollup
End of each month

Totals all weeks in the month. Your CPA reconciles books monthly. Also useful for seeing if your tax reserve account is keeping up.

Includes: All weekly exports rolled up · Monthly gross per worker · Monthly WH totals · Running YTD start
